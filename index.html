BIGGER FISH RULES:

- Robot, AI, PC will receive a random amount of coins non-disclosed even to themselves or opponent

- Users will receive a random amount of coins only disclosed to them

- There is no time limit to make a decision or for total duration of the game. If you wish to quit, you can always bet your full balance or press the "fold" button

- Current version supports only direct match against a computer

- Your score will be reset when you refresh or close this tab where game runs

- UI

- If at any time you want to exit the game screen or start a new game, you can do it by refreshing the page

- Winning results lead to an option of continuing game

- Winning results lead to an option of game restart

- Folding results lead to both next game and new game
